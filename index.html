About Me

Hi there, welcome to my website! If you are new here, allow me to introduce myself. My name is James Daly and I have a long & prestigious background in computer programming. My interest in programming and computers in general was initially discovered when I was exploring different classes to take in high school. I was unsure at the time what my plans would be after completing school but knew firsthand that technology was something I was particularly fond of. So I then began enrolling in a variety of computer-related courses which I excelled in and acquired further experiences from, including learning about the Big Three languages, effectively designing websites, and understanding the fundamentals of integrated technology. I also supplemented this well-rounded tech experience by participating in my school's First Robotics Competition team where I gained a broader coding experience by helping program an autonomous robot that would go on to win regional and state championships. In continuing to partake in many courses and activities involving programming, I hope to diversly enhance my education of and appreciation for the world of programming.
